Getting Started

How To Use BitGuard

BitGuard helps you quickly assess the risk of a Bitcoin wallet address using graph-based analysis and structured risk signals.

How it works

Follow these steps to run your first wallet assessment.

  1. Enter a Bitcoin Address On the homepage, use the search bar to enter the Bitcoin address you want to assess.
  2. Select Depth Choose a depth from 0 to 3 to control how many layers of connected transaction activity are analyzed.
  3. Submit for Assessment Click the Analyze button to send the address for risk evaluation.
  4. View Results Review the risk score, confidence level, and recommendation returned by the model.
