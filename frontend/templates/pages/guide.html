How To Use BitGuard

Using BitGuard is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Enter a Bitcoin Address: On the homepage, you'll find a search bar. Input any Bitcoin address you want to assess.
  2. Select Depth: Choose the depth of analysis (0-3) to determine how many layers of transaction history to evaluate.
  3. Submit for Assessment: Click the search button to submit the address for risk assessment.
  4. View Results: After processing, you'll receive a risk score, predicted type, confidence level, and recommendations based on the analysis.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us through our Contact Us page.
